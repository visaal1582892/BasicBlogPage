Harry Potter

Story Plot

The Theme of complete Harry Potter franchise revolves around the existence of magical world without being known by the muggles aka normal humans. The Story mainly revolves around the two main characters Harry Potter and The Voldemort. This franchise shows us how an innocent and scared little boy became the most courageous one to defeat one of the darkest and powerful wizards of that time.

Key Highlights

My favourite characters in this franchise are Harry Potter, Ron Weasely, Harmione Granger, Voldemort, Hargrid, Severous Snape who literally given life to those characters. They uncover the mysteries of the wizarding world, battle dark forces led by Lord Voldemort, and discover the power of love, friendship, and courage. Themes of loyalty and sacrifice underpin their quest to defeat evil. The series blends magical adventures with deep emotional growth, making it a timeless tale.

Resident Evil

Story Plot

In Resident Evil Franchise, Complete story plot happens at end of the world period, where almost all the human race is at the edge of extinction due a dangerous virus.The only organization left isUmbrella Corporation , which tries to erase entire world and start a new generation under them. The main character Alice tries to stop this. The franchise starts with an unknown person leaking the virus by entering into an underground vault. The climax of the franchise will be amazing with a big twist.

Key Highlights

My favourite characters in this franchise are Alice, Red Queen(An AI) , Matt Addison, Parks and rain Ocampo. who literally given life to those characters. Packed with intense action, survival horror, and mutated creatures, the films explore humanity's struggle against extinction. Alice's journey uncovers secrets about her own past and the corporation's dark agenda. The series blends fast-paced combat with dystopian storytelling, creating a thrilling cinematic experience.

Maze Runner

Story Plot

In Maze Runner the main happens at the time of extinction of the human race due to a dangerous virus named Flare. The main story plot revolves around the young and brave Thomas and WICKD, The organization which tries to find cure by sacrificing the younger generation and extraction a serum from their blood which is the gift of evolution in younger generation. Thomas tries to revolt against this organization with some of his friends. This franchise has one of the most intresting climax scenes. I recommend everyone to enjoy this franchise.

Key Highlights

My favourite characters in this franchise are Thomas, Newt, Fry, Minho, Gally, Teresa and Chuck who literally given life to those characters. Filled with intense action and suspense, the films explore themes of survival, friendship, and rebellion against oppressive systems. As they face treacherous trials, including dangerous creatures and betrayal, the group fights for freedom and truth. The series combines gripping dystopian storytelling with emotional depth and thrilling twists.
